# Project End Goal

## Business Goal

The primary goal of this project is to build an **Employee Attrition Prediction and Retention Strategy Dashboard** that helps organizations identify attrition risks and take preventive actions.

---

## Analytical Goals

The project aims to:
- Analyze historical employee attrition trends
- Identify key drivers of employee turnover
- Segment employees based on attrition risk
- Measure the impact of satisfaction, performance, and tenure on attrition

---

## Final Deliverables

At the end of this project, the following outputs will be available:

- Cleaned and well-structured employee datasets
- Attrition-related KPIs such as:
  - Attrition Rate
  - Average Tenure of Employees Who Left
  - Department-wise Attrition
- Interactive Power BI dashboards
- Clear business insights and recommendations for retention strategies

---

## Value to Business

This project enables HR teams to:
- Detect early warning signs of attrition
- Focus retention efforts on high-risk employee groups
- Improve workforce stability using data-backed decisions

The dashboard is designed for **non-technical stakeholders**, ensuring insights are easy to understand and act upon.
